Visier's Job and Skills Ontology

Visier's job and skills ontology is an API-based platform for strategic workforce planning, jo matching, learning, and recruitment. The platform takes your data and returns actionable insights on an organization or employee level.

Project Objective

Demonstrate the value and potential of Visier's job matching capabilities to talent acquisition roles and to job seekers. Facilitate the collection of emails and conduct an information transaction to help build Visier's baseline dataset.

I led the design process from concept development, research, and testing through to the final product—a cross-platform card reading game to match talent aquisition and job seekers to skill profiles.
