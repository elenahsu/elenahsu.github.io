I am an experience and visual designer passionate in crafting positive experiences and constructing valuable human-centric solutions because I believe in a community that interculturally connects human beings to one another. Adventure-ready, I actively explore new design approaches while striving for engaging and effective solutions through design, communication, and storytelling.

Being an avid traveler and being invited into new cultures has not only shaped my own personal perspective but also what it means for me to design. In my own personal life and career, I want to continuously push myself to not get too comfortable, to challenge routines, and to seek new experiences. I love how design can influence change, particularly what inspires and influences my work are purposeful designs for people—for different perspectives, cultures, and communities. I design for a future that is more thoughtful, more empathetic, and more accessible.

I am currently based in Vancouver, Canada.
