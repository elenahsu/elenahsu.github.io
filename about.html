Huhlo, it's me!

I'm Elena,

a senior at Simon Fraser University currently majoring in Interactive Arts and Technology and concentrating in interaction design.

I am a experience designer interested in crafting positive experiences and constructing valuable user-centric solutions because I believe in a community that interculturally connects human beings to one another. Adventure-ready, I like to actively explore new design approaches while striving for engaging and effective solutions through design, communication, and storytelling.

A few things about me, I am perpetually curious and easily excited, I love to experience new cultures and travel, I am a firm believer of the Oxford comma, and I have a strong dislike for lemon squares.

I just came home from a semester abroad in Hilversum, Netherlands at MediaMonks working as a UX intern, and is currently based in Vancouver, Canada for the semester.
