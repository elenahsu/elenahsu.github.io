I'm Elena (she/her)— a digital designer specialized in UX and visual design, passionate in crafting valuable solutions that interculturally connect people together.

☻   A few quick things about me:

01.  Adventure-ready, I love to experience new cultures,

02.  I have a strong dislike for lemon squares, and

03.  I design for people.

Being an avid traveller and being invited into new cultures has not only shaped my own personal perspective but also what it means for me to design. In my own personal life and career, I want to continuously push myself to not get too comfortable, to challenge routines, and to seek new experiences. I love how design can influence change. What inspires and influences my work are purposeful designs for people—for different perspectives, cultures, and communities. I design for a future that is more thoughtful, more empathetic, and more accessible.

I'm currently based in Vancouver, Canada.
