The Peak is an independent SFU student newspaper. As part of the production team employee, I organize templates and design full-page coloured layouts while collaborating with other editors and assistants using the Adobe Creative Suite.

Moving Forward

For two semesters I have had the pleasure of working at The Peak while collaborating with other editors and production team members. A valuable lesson I learned in effective communication is to not only always design with real content, but to also always design with a stance. The Peak has helped me build on my foundation of visual communication and taught me a lot about print design while giving me the freedom to explore different design styles.
