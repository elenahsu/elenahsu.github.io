UrbanLogiq

For governments of all sizes, UrbanLogiq helps public officials make better, data-driven decisions to build safer and more equitable communities. Leveraging advanced data analytics and AI-driven capabilities, UrbanLogiq transforms data into actionable insights that support a wide range of government use cases, such as traffic and transportation planning, road safety, fire prevention, and economic development.

Reimagining data for communities

From conceptualization to the final product, I collaborate closely with industry experts and governments to develop data solutions that educate and empower our clients. I design to simplify government workflows and introduce the latest research and best practices to help create safer communities.

At UrbanLogiq, I led the transportation workspace overhaul to offer better data exploration capabilities through flexible data integration. This includes curated default datasets and map interactions with suggested follow-up prompts to guide users to the data they need right away. Reports have been designed to now provide clear, tailored insights that cater to our client’s specific needs and decision-making processes. From maps to visualizations, I’ve introduced a new visual language that ensures accessibility and usability are prioritized throughout the platform.

The overhaul has set the design foundation for future products at UrbanLogiq.

Currently, I'm working on tools to help analyze road crashes and streamline First Nations land transfers. If you would like to know more, let's grab a coffee and chat more.
