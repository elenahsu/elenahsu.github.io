Touchy Subjects

Touchy Subjects is a sexual health and wellness platform, primarily targeted as those that identify as men. Informing yourself and creating a healthy perspective of sex and sexual identity is nothing to be embarrassed about– ask those “weird” questions, get the resources and knowledge you need, or find extra help– whatever it is, we’re here, no matter the subject.

Brief

This year's theme for XDHacks is sustainable health. My team and I chose to the free path route.

Vision

a sexual health and wellness platform, primarily targeted as those that identify as men. Informing yourself and creating a healthy perspective of sex and sexual identity is nothing to be embarrassed about– ask those “weird” questions, get the resources and knowledge you need, or find extra help– whatever it is, we’re here, no matter the subject.

Domain

Sexual health in youths and adolescents.

Currently, thriving areas in this domain include women centric health and resources, parent targeted aid, and general view and understanding of sexual health in youth and adolescents.

Opportunity

There’s a need for a men’s space in the sector because men are taught to be inherently dominant, constantly sexual, and most of all– totally in the know.

Precendents

Solution

www.touchysubjects.org

We are providing an all-inclusive safe space geared towards men (and those that identify as men)to start their sexual perspective with a strong foundation. A healthy, respective, and informed sexual foundation is the key to sustainable physical and mental health as well as confidence.

Design Principles

Be Professional

Being professional in visuals and imagery, Touchy Subjects reflects a relatable, youthful, and laid back environment without resorting to childish clipart or stock images.

Be Inclusive

Touchy Subjects aims to reflect an inclusive perspective. Advice and resources that are given are as all- encompassing as possible, and stays away from heteronormative-centric only advice.

Be Clear

Our platform is meant to educate our users quickly and enjoyably. Touchy Subjects “tells it like it is” and doesn’t stray away from the embarrassing or awkward.

Copywriting

The language used on Touchy Subjects is an integral feature on the platform. All of the original content has been written to be as informative as possible while still using words that are relatable and easy to read. Touchy Subjects takes pride in also being thoughtful and professional to the reader. It does not try and “fluff” up the content with hyper- masculine assumptions— eg. “you might feel this way after your hard football practice.” Our language is objective, short, and to the point.

Intro to Sex Guide

A key feature on our website, the sex guide is especially aimed at those who are curious about sex, having sex for the first time, or about to start having sex regularly. It aims to “myth-bust” popular opinions about sex, and educate and inform the newly sexually active about healthy practices.

Based on the first few questions, the quiz will return a set of questions best tailored to your needs. Depending on what you answer, a flash-card style guide will be curated with advice especially for you. This guide will link to more in-depth resources if the user wishes.

Consent

If the user is unfamiliar with consent and what it entails, a page detailing the importance of consent will display. This page provides examples on how to ensure active consent and how to watch for an individual that may not be consenting.

What is Consent? (n.d.). Retrieved from https://www.loveisrespect.org/healthy- relationships/what-consent/

Sustainable Sexual Health Coast-to-Coast

We would see this platform being as standard point of reference for high school to university health resources, potentially broadening the scope from British Columbia to the rest of Canada.
